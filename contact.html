Contact us

Currently the Redlink Platform is under private beta testing. Therefore, for accessing the API, via the SDK, plugins or custom calls, you must require an invitation. If you could be interested to join the private beta program, please sign-up explaining your use case, and we will evaluate your proposal as soon as possible. That's because in this early phase we really want to work together with you, trying to understand your needs and see what/how we can address.

If you have any technical issue about our API, please drop us your questions to support@redlink.io and we will be answering as soon as we possibly can. All questions sent to that email address remain completely confidential between you and our support team.

In addition, if you would have any other general question about us, please contact us. To find out about the newest updates, launches, events and news, check our blog or follow @RedLinkGmbH on twitter.
